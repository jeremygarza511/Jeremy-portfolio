Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in entry-level software development and IT support opportunities where I can apply both my hands-on technical experience and my formal education in software engineering. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering at Western Governors University and actively building the skills needed to transition into a full-time technical role.

My professional background might look unconventional for a developer candidate, but I believe it sets me apart. As a Sales Planning Lead at IKEA, I worked daily with Salesforce CRM and Salja Pro, managing data pipelines, diagnosing system inconsistencies, and supporting one of only 10 IKEA-in-Best Buy pilot locations in the country. That role taught me how to think like a systems thinker inside a real operational environment — not just in a classroom. I learned to keep data clean, processes moving, and people supported, all at the same time.

Before IKEA, I spent nearly two years at Best Buy developing sharp customer communication skills and an intuitive understanding of consumer technology. Working the sales floor at a tech retailer gave me direct exposure to the products and systems that everyday users interact with — a perspective I carry into every technical task I approach. I think about the person on the other end of the software, not just the code.

Beyond work, I volunteer as an IT support technician at Windemere Nursing Home, where I troubleshoot hardware and software issues for staff and residents. Communicating technical solutions to non-technical users in a high-stakes, care-focused environment has sharpened both my patience and my ability to diagnose problems quickly and explain them clearly.

On my own time, I maintain a personal home lab where I have configured Linux on dedicated hardware and experiment with networking, virtualization, and system administration. This self-directed learning reflects how I approach my career.

I am eager to bring this combination of real-world experience, growing technical skill, and genuine curiosity to a team that values motivated, adaptable contributors. I would welcome the opportunity to speak with you and can be reached at (210) 718-8285 or jeremygarza311@gmail.com at your convenience. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
